Who Am I?

I'm originally from Bulgaria but now based in Miami, Florida. I had previously lived in the UK for almost a decade where I graduated and had most of my work experience.

Although my professional path has taken many twists and turns — from academic writing and research, to pharmacy employee, to a coordinator role at an online travel business (where I first developed my interest in coding) — I've never stopped trying to create meaningful value for others.

And this is where web development comes in, I enjoy challenges, problem solving and ultimately see my work efforts turn into useful applications for individuals and businesses alike.
